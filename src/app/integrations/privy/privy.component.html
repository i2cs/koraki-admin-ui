Privy Integration

This integrations lets you generate notifications from your Privy campaign related events. At the moment Koraki can generate notifications when a lead signs up for a campaign.

Instructions

How to install
Log in to your Privy account and go to Settings > Integrations. Select Webhooks on the left side navigation, click Custom then change the Method dropdown to "POST". Copy the above unique URL (don't share it with anyone) and paste on "URL" field. Select Signups checkbox from the Triggers and Save. Click on Test button to register the integration.
How to uninstall
Log in to your Privy account and go to Settings > Integrations. Select Webhooks on the left side navigation. Click on the delete button of the Custom webhook which has URL starting with https://api.koraki.io
