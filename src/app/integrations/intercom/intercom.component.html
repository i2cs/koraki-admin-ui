Intercom Integration

This integrations provides capability to generate social notifications on actions made within intercom app. This integration can show lead and contact sign up events realtime.

Instructions

Log in to your Intercom account and go to Developer hub > Webhooks.
Click on Create webhook button. Copy and paste the above URL on "Webhook URL" field. Copy and paste provided secret (5 letter code) on Hub secret field. Check Lead created and User created checkboxes and click on Create webhook button. After the webhook successfully created, click on Ping button to complete the integration.
How to uninstall
Log in to your Intercom account and go to Developer hub > Webhooks. Click the delete button of the webhook which has URL starting with https://api.koraki.io
